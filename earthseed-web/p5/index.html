welcome to earthseed










you have arrived at the gate.
thank you for your curiosity. it has taken many moons and suns and tears and laughs
and rage across the generations to collect ourselves and arrive to build earthseed,
a place where we can rest our stories.

my name is dre and their is ximena and you have entered our earthseed territory,
a place where we have begun constructing and reconstructing what we know about our land/body stories.
the regular rules of the internet and open access do not apply here.

to enter earthseed, guests must comply by our protocols
and commitments to indigenous landback struggles across the world.
